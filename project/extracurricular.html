1- Authored a scientific research paper focusing on the role

of the MAPK pathway in cancer development.

Got accepted into Lumiere Program, a scientific individual research program with 100% financial aid that covered the $2600. I was one of 120 out of 324 who got accepted into 5 months winter cohort program after filling out the application and interview filtration.

Through my extensive research, I have thoroughly explored numerous past and ongoing clinical trials that investigate drugs targeting RTK mutations, providing valuable insights into the potential future of cancer treatment. Within my research paper, I have specifically highlighted the paramount importance of c-Myc and Ras proteins in the MAPK pathway. Particularly, I have identified c-Myc as a pivotal protein capable of inducing metabolic alterations in transformed cells, with its mutation resulting in the formation of malignant tumors.
